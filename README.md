# The Gottdank Family Tree

An interactive family tree tracing the rare Gottdank surname — from Gittel & Coleman in Galicia,
through Charles the New York barber, down to today's family in the US and Canada.

**Live site:** once Pages is turned on, it'll be at
`https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/gottdank-tree/`

## What's here
- `index.html` — the whole tree. One self-contained file, no dependencies. Open it locally
  by double-clicking, or host it anywhere.

## How it's sourced
Cards are shaded by confidence:
- **solid** — verified against public records (obituaries, the Social Security Death Index, FamilySearch)
- **lighter / dashed** — established by family documents or inference, awaiting a primary record

Built from two family documents (Jordan's hand-drawn chart and the "Gottdank Descendants" note)
plus several rounds of public-records research.

*A living document — corrections from family are very welcome.*
